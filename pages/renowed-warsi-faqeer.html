Sarkar Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Mai Ye Ek Aise Fuqra Hai Jinko Sarkar Ne Ehramposhi Toh Door Mureed Karne Se Bhi Pehle Aapko Laqab Se Nawaza "Gulab Shah". Inka Waqia Kuch Iss Tarah Ye Talib Hone Ke Liye Bhut Bechain The Innke Dost Aur Ye Dono Gaye Kisi Buzurg Se Mureed Hone. Wo Buzurg Ne Unke Dost Ko Toh Mureed Kar Liya Aur Gulab Shah Ko Kaha Aapka Hissa Mere Paas Nahi Hai. Jinka Hissa Aapke Paas Hai Wo Aapko Dhoond Lege. Gulab Shah Ko Yahi Fikar Rehne Lagi Ki Hum Bhi Kisi Se Mureed Ho Jate, Hamara Bhi Koi Peer Hota Toh Hum Raahe Nijat Paaa Lete.Itne Mai Ye Shehre Mai Nikle Aur Dekha Ki Ek Gufa Ke Andar Bhut Zyda Andera Hai Aur Waha Se Bhut Zyda Khusboo Aa Rahi Hai. Itne Mai Gufa Ke Andar Gaye Aur Dekha Ki Andere Mai Sarkar Waris Jalwa Afroz Hai Aur Andere Mai Sarkar Waris Ka Noor Jagmaga Raha Hai, Itne Mai Hi Sarkar Waris Ne Kaha Gulab Shah Aa Gaye Tum Toh Hamare Gulab Shah.Subhan Allah.

Jab Se Ye Kehna Tha Tum Gulab Shah Ho, Abhi Mureed Bhi Nahi Kiya, Abhi Ehramposh Bhi Nahi Kiya. Itne Mai Aapke Badan Se Gulab Ki Khushboo Aane Lagi. Baad Mai Sarkar Ne Aapko Mureed Kiya Aur Ehramposh Kiya {Libase Faqr Ataa Kiya}

Gulab Shah Warsi Ka Laqab Ataa Kiya. Gulab Shah Warsi Rematullah Alaih Aapke Pehle Mureed Aur Ehramposh Faqeer Hai.

Gulab Shah Warsi Rehmatullah Alaih Ka Mujaida Ye Hai Ki Aap 44 Saal Tak Soo Hi Nahi.

Sarkar Waris Paak Ne Farmaya Gulab Shah Gulab Shah Iss Darakh Ke Niche Baith Jaao Aur Aankhe Khole Rakho,Jo Kuch Khuda Dikhaye Wo Dekhte Raho. Uss Bandaye Khuda Ne Uss Gulab Shah Rehmatullah Alaih Ki Zaat Pe Lakho Salam.

Unhone Apne Peer Ki Baat Par Amal Karte Hue Uss Darakh Ke Niche Baith Gaye Aur 44 Saal Tak Soye Hi Nahi, Apni Aankhe Kholi Rakhi.

Jo Aqle Insani Se Door Door Tak Pata Nahi Milta Aise Aise Mujaide Sarkar Waris Paak Ne Apne Mureedo Se Karwaye Hai.Science Kehti Hai 3 Din Pyaas Se Reh Sakta Hai Aur 5 Ya 7 Din Tak Begair Khaaye Reh Sakte Hai, Lekin Begair Soye 72 Ghanto Se Zyda Nahi Reh Sakta Uski Maut Ho Jagey Aur Gulab Shah 44 Saal Tak Aankhe Kholi Rakhi, Soye Hi Nahi.Yaha Par Medical Science Ki Theory Fail Ho Gayi Aur Isi Ko Kehte Hai Mujaida.

Sarkar bhut khush hue aur apne khaas Mureed ko bhut Duaein di. Sarkar ne Hafiz Gulab Shah Warsi ke Ishq ko dekh kar kaha tha ke Gulab Shah tumhari 7 Peedhiya humse mureed hai

Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ne Gulab Shah ko farmay ki Pani bata karo jo tumhare hath se Pani piyenga usse Shifa'at Mil Jaegay.

Hafiz Gulab Shah Warsi pani phoonk kar dete the. Pani pine wala Shifa paa jata tha. Aapke waha Jawan, Buddhe, Baccho ki bheed lagi rehti thi. Darwaze par Rogiyo, Dukhiyo ka Tanta bana rehta tha. 8 10 bhishti pani liye khade rehte the aur mitti ke thokre mai pani bhjete the. Ittefaq se Collector Sahab Bahadur ki patni ko prasav peeda (pregnancy mai dard) hui, do chaar din isi taqleef aur pareshani mai guzar gaye. Doctor bhi pareshan ho gaye. Logo ne Jilla ke mahoday se kaha yaha par ek Faqeer rehte hai wo har beemari ke liye pani phoonk kar dete hai. Collector Sahab ne pani maanga kar istemal kiya. Bacha paida hua. Collector sahab ne bade sahab se maang kar panch hazar aamdani ek moje ki sanad banakar tehsildaar sahab ke hath se bheja. Hafiz Gulab Shah lene mai sochne lage aur isi mamle mai unhone ek khat (letter) Sarkar Waris Paak ki seva mai bheja ki jo aapki ijazat ho kiya jaaye. Aapne Gulab Shah ko hukum kiya ki Gulab Shah khud hi inkaar kar dena jaruri tha jo sab beemari ko door kar sakta hai, Wo bhook, pyaas ki taqleef ko bhi bardash kar sakta hai. Jo Lalach mai gir jaaye wo hamara nahi. Gulab Shah ne sanad lene se inkaar kar diya. Collector sahab udaas ho uthe aur kuch soch samaj kar Hafiz Sahab ke makan se lag kat ek masjid bana di.

Sarkar Alampanah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu farmate hai ~ Gulab Shah Na Tama Karo, Na Mana Karo, Na Jama Karo.

Huwal Warisul Kareem.
