Quraan O Hadees Ki tashreeh krte hue, Sarkar Alampanah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Farmate Hai~

1. NAMAZ WAQT PAR ADA KARNA AFZAL AUR FARMABARDARI KI DALEEL HAI.

Aye Iman Walo Tum Par Roze Farz Kiye Gaye Hai Jaise Pehle Logo Par Farz Kiye Gaye The Taki Tum Parhezgar Bano

{Surah Baqarah Ayat 183}

Sarkar Aalampanaah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Farmate Hai~

1. SHOK SE ROZA RAKHNA AASHIQO KI SUNNAT HAI.

2. ROZA RAKHNE SE KHUDA KI MOHABBAT BADHTI HAI.

3. ROZA AISI QADR IBADAT HAI KI ROZEDAAR BANDE KO KHUDA APNE DOSTO MAI SHUMAAR KARTA HAI.

4. ROZA RAKHNE SE NAFS MAGHLOOB {Surrender} HOTA HAI.

5. ROZA GUNAHO KO MITATA HAI.

Roze Ka Asal Maqsad Hi Hasile Parhezgari Hai, Allah Yahi Chahta Hai Ke Uske Bande Parhezgar Ban Jaaye Aur Parhezgari Tabhi Hasil Hoti Hai Jab Banda Gunaho Ka Rasta Chhod Kar Uski Taraf Mutawwajo Hota Hai. Yani Jab Banda Gunaho Se Taiyb Ho Jata Hai Tab Parhezgaro Mai Shumaar Hota Hai {Jo Ke Bhut Mushkil Hai Par Na Mumkin Nahi} Aur Gunaho Ka Rasta Banda Tabhi Chor Sakta Hai Jab Uska Nafs Maghloob Ho Jaaye Yani Nafse Ammara Se Rukhsat Ho Kar Uska Safar Nafse Mutmainna Tak Pahuch Jaaye Aur Ye Saari Nemate Usko Roze Ke Zariye Hasil Hoti Hai.Islye Yahi Taleeme Warsiyat Hai Ke Roza Rakhne Se Nafse Ammara Se Chhutkara Milta Hai Aur Fir Khuda Ka Qurb Hasil Hota Hai.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.

Hazrat Maulana Sirajul Yaqeen Sahab ke bete Hazrat Maulana Sadiqul Yaqeen sahab jo Hazrat Nijatullah Shah Qadri Rahmatullah Alaih ke Gaddi Nasheen the Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ke baare mein accha Gumaan nahi rakhte the. Saath hi apne Shagirdo ko Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki bargah mein jaane se mana karte the. Maulana Sadiqul Yaqeen Sahab Ilm Hasil karne ke liye Baghdad Sharif tashreef le gaye, wahan kaafi saal rahe. Fir wahan se 7 saal Makkah Mukarrama aur Madina Munavvara mein qayam kiya. Fir jab Hindustan aane ka irada kiya toh pehle Deva Sharif Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki khidmat mein hazir hue aur Sarkar ki Qadambosi ki. Log hairat mein the ke Maulana sahab toh Sarkar ke baare mein accha Gumaan nahi rakhte the, fir achanak se Sarkar se itni Muhabbat kaise ho gayi? Jab logo ne Maulana Sadiqul Yaqeen Sahab se poocha toh unhone bataya ke main Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ke Buland Maqam se bey khabar tha. Maine 7 saal tak Masjid-E-Haram aur Masjid-E-Nabvi mein Namaz padhi aur har Namaz mein maine apni Seedhi taraf Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ko Namaz Padhte Paaya. Yeh meri Aankho Dekhi baat hai. Iske baad maine apne Gumaan se Tauba kar li.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Jinn

Jinn

×

Baghdad Sharif mein Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ka ek mureed rehta tha jiski Biwi par har Itwar ek jinn sawar hota tha. Bohot Ilaj, dua karne ke baad bhi use koi rahat na mili. Jab Sarkar Waris-E-Paak Baghdad Sharif tashreef laaye toh woh mureed Aapki bargah mein hazir hua aur apni takleef Sarkar se bayan ki aur makan par tashreef laane ki guzarish ki. Ittefaq se woh itwar ka hi din tha. Jab Sarkar uske Makan par Tashreef le gaye toh jinn uss Aurat par Sawar hi tha. Sarkar ne uss Jinn se poocha ke woh kyu har Itwar iss Aurat par kyu Sawar hota hai, toh usne jawab diya ke main iss Aurat ka Aashiq hu, isi liye isse milne har Itwar ko aata hu. Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ne uss Jinn se irshad farmaya ke agar saccha Aashiq hai toh har Itwar ko hi kyu aata hai, har Roz aaya kar. Sarkar ke yeh Qalme sunn kar Jinn ne kaha ke aisa Ishq mujhe Manzoor nahi, main jaa raha hu aur ab kabhi nahi aaunga. Uske baad se woh Aurat bilkul theek ho gayi aur Sarkar ki Mureed ho gayi. Uss waqt Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki Umr Sharif 14 saal thi.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.

Sabr

Sabr

×

Sarkar Waris-E-Pak ek martaba paidal safar mein the. Khuch arsa paidal chalne ke baad ek oont (Camel) par ek Maulvi sahab aaye, woh bhi safar mein the. Maulvi sahab Sarkar se kehne lage ke Haji Sahab badi garmi hai, Aaiye mere oont par baith jaiye, Sarkar ne kaha nahi Hum paida jayenge. Bahr haal unke bohot zidd karne ke baad Sarkar oont par sawar ho gaye. Maulvi sahab bohot zor zor se "Innalaha Ma'as Sabireen" (Allah sabr karne walo ke saath hai) ka wird kar rahe the. Sarkar Waris-E-Pak ne kaha ke Maulvi sahab kya padh rahe ho? Maulvi sahab gusse mein kehte hai ke Haji Sahab ajeeb baat hai, Qur'an ki itni mashoor ayat tumko pata nahi? Innalaha Ma'as Sabireen, yeh sabr ki ayat hai, main iska wird humesha karta hu. Sarkar ne farmaya bohot acchi baat hai. Kyunki garmi bohot thi toh paani khatm ho gaya. Pyaas ke maare Maulvi sahab gusse mein 'Paani, Paani' chillane lage. Jab Maulvi sahab Sarkar ke paas aake paani paani chillane lage toh Sarkar ne muskura kar farmaya "Innalaha Ma'as Sabireen" , Maulvi sahab yeh sabr ki ayat hai. Yeh sunn kar Maulvi sahab ko uss ayat ka asl matlab samajh mein aaya aur Sarkar ke qadmo mein sar rakh diya. Farman-E-Waris Pak: Suna Suna Sabr O Raza Ka Martaba Jisko Bhi Mila Ahle Bayth Ke Ghar Se Mila.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Moharram ke 10 dino mai Aap Marsiya bhi sunte the aur Ahle Bayth ki Veerta bhi sunte the. Lekin jab koi Ben {Rodan ya Rota} Karta toh aap kehte yeh Galat hai. Wo Tasleem Raza pe the, aisa nahi hua. Ye sab toh Rone Rulane ke liye banaye hai.

Ishq

Ek Shakhs ne Huzur Qibla Sarkar Alampanah Huzur Waris-E-Pak Azamallaho Zikruhu se Lucknow mein Arz kiya ke Maine Tamam Umr fuzuliyaat mein zaaya ki aur Jawani ke ayyaam mein bohat Na-Shaaista Harkatein ki ab siwaye Hasrat aur Ashk-E-Nidamat ke mere paas kuch nahi, Khuda ke liye Dua farmaiye aur Mujhe Raahe Raast dikha dijiye. Huzur Alampanah Waris-E-Panjatan ne Muskurate hue farmaya "ISHQ KA KHEL KHELO" Us shakhs ne arz ki ke Main nahi Janta Ishq kya cheez hai aur Aashiq kaun hota hai? Agar Ishq se Muraad Aurton aur Mardo se Muhabbat hai to ye Maine Baar-Ha kiya magar siwaye Dunya aur Aakhirat ke Nuqsan ke kuch Hasil na hua. Is par Huzur Anwar Waris-E-Pak A'alallaho Muqamuhu ne farmaya ke agar Tum Ishq hi nahi jante to Ishq-Bazi kaise kar sakte ho? Usne jawab diya ke main isi liye pareshan hun aur iska koi ilaaj nahi janta . Huzur Alampanah Waris-E-Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu ne irshad farmaya : Ishq Teen Hurf se Murakkab hai "Ayn" "Sheen" "Qaaf". "Ayn" Se Ishara Hai Ibadat E Zahiri O Batini Karo. "Sheen" Se Ishara Hai Ke Kamal E Zauq O Shauq Se Shari'at E Mutah-Hara Ki Pabandi Karo. Turkey Ka Safar

Turkey Ka Safar

×

Jab Sarkar Waris-E-Paak Turkey ke safar mein the Aap Abdullah Hajab Warsi {Jo Makkah Mukarrama mein Aapse mureed hue the} ke ghar ruke the. Uss waqt Satlanat-E-Usmaniya ke 31st khalifa Hazrat Sultan Abdul Majeed Khan ki hukumat thi. Abdullah Sahab shahi darbar mein kaam karte the aur Sultan tak unki rasai thi. Al garz Abdullah Sahab israr kar ke Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ko shahi baug ki sair ke liye le gaye. Sarkar baug mein baithe hi the ke itne mein Sultan Abdul Majeed Khan ki shahi sawari aayi aur poore roab ke saath Sarkar ke saamne ruk gayi. Sultan hairat mein the ke ek ehramposh faqeer itni bey niyazi se shahi baug mein baitha hai. Sultan abhi hairat mein hi the ke Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ne use ek nazar dekha aur woh seedha Sarkar ke qadmo mein gir pade. Sultan ne Abdullah Sahab se poocha ke yeh buzurg kaun aur kahan thehre hai? Abdullah sahab ke jawab dene se pehle hi Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ne Turkish zuban mein farmaya "Faqeer ka koi ghar nahi aur sab ghar Faqeer ke". Sultan ne hairat se poocha ke Aap Turkish zuban bhi jaante hai? Sarkar ne farmaya ke Humein Dil ki Zuban Aati Hai aur Jise Dil ki Zuban Aati Hai woh Sab Zubanein Jaanta hai. Sultan laa jawab ho chuke the, sab ameer, wazeer, ghulam hairan pareshan the ke yeh kis paaye ka Faqeer hai jiske saamne Arab wa Ajam ka Sultan bey bus khada hai. Sultan ne kaanpte larazte hue Sarkar se shahi mahal mein aane ki darkhwast ki, Sarkar ne dawat qubool ki aur doosre din shahi mahal mein aane ka waada kiya. "Shahi Mahal Mein Sarkar Ka Shahi Istaqbal" Doosre din jab Sarkar Waris Paak Sultan Abdul Majeed ke mahal {Topkapi Palace} mein tashreef le gaye tab nazara dekhne layak tha. Sultan ka poora Khandan, wWzeer, Ghulam aur Mahal ke tamam log andar se bahar tak Saff Dar Saff Gardan Jhukaye Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ke Istaqbal ke liye Khade the. Al garz, jab Sarkar tashreef laaye toh pehle toh Sultan Abdul Majeed Khan aur unke Khandan wale Mureed hue. Uske baad mahal ke tamam Wazeer, Ghulam aur Mahal ke baaki log Aapke Dast-E-Haq par Bai'at hue. Ab sheher mein Sultan ki iradatmaandi aur Sharf-E-Ghulami hasil karne ki khabar mashoor hui toh mahal ke neeche hazaro logo ka hujoom lag gaya. Har shakhs Aqeedatmandi ke josh mein ghulami hasil karne ke liye Bey chain nazar aata. Hujoom ki kasrat ke Pesh-E-Nazar Sarkar ne hukm diya ke mahal ke neeche ek rassi laga di jaaye aur ailan kiya jaaye ke jo iss rassi ko choo lega woh Humara Mureed hai. Rassi lagate hi Majma Bey Kaabu ho gaya toh ghulamo ne arz kiya ke Sarkar! Muhabbat ka yeh tamasha kahi Khoon Rezi mein na badal jaaye, yeh sunn kar Sarkar Waris-E-Paak jharokhe {Balcony} mein tashreef laaye aur aqeedatmando ko apne chaand jaise chehre ka deedar karate hue farmaya "Jisne Humein dekh liya woh Humara mureed ho gaya". Iss tarah logo ne Sarkar ke Chehra-E-Anwar ka deedar kiya aur aapas mein ek doosre se gale mil ke mubarakbaad dene lage goya eid ka din ho. Sultan ke israr karne par Aapne yahan ek hafta qayam farmaya. Dauran-E-Qayam roz hazaro log Aapse milne aur faiz hasil karne aate.
