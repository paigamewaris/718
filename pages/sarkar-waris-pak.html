YAQEENAN NAMAZ MOMINEEN PAR MUQARRARAH {Specified} WAQTO PAR FARZ HAI.

{Surah Nisa-Ayat-103}

Hazrat Abdullah Ibne Masood R.A Farmate Hai Ke Maine Huzoor Paak ﷺ se daryaft kiya k konsa Amal Allah Ko zyada pasand hai to Aap ﷺ ne irshad farmaya~

WAQT PAR NAMAZ PADHNA

{SAHI BUKHARI}

Quraan O Hadees Ki tashreeh krte hue, Sarkar Alampanah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Farmate Hai~

1. NAMAZ WAQT PAR ADA KARNA AFZAL AUR FARMABARDARI KI DALEEL HAI.

2. NAMAZ ME AMADAN DER KARNA KAHILI KI DALEEL HAI AUR MALIK K HUKM ME KAHILI ABDIYAT K MANAFI {Contradiction} HAI.

NOTE- NAMAZ APNE MUQARRAR WAQT PE FARZ HAI ISLIYE USKO WAQT PE ADA KARNA AFZAL HAI AUR KHUDA KO YE PASAND HAI AUR ASHIQ KA MANSAB HI YAHI HAI K EHKAME MASHOOQ K AAGE SARE TASLEEM KHAM RAHE ISLIYE TALEEM-E-WARSIYAT BHI YAHI HAI.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal Warisul Kareem
