YAQEENAN NAMAZ MOMINEEN PAR MUQARRARAH {Specified} WAQTO PAR FARZ HAI.

{Surah Nisa-Ayat-103}

Hazrat Abdullah Ibne Masood R.A Farmate Hai Ke Maine Huzoor Paak ﷺ se daryaft kiya k konsa Amal Allah Ko zyada pasand hai to Aap ﷺ ne irshad farmaya~

WAQT PAR NAMAZ PADHNA

{SAHI BUKHARI}

Quraan O Hadees Ki tashreeh krte hue, Sarkar Alampanah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Farmate Hai~

1. NAMAZ WAQT PAR ADA KARNA AFZAL AUR FARMABARDARI KI DALEEL HAI.

2. NAMAZ ME AMADAN DER KARNA KAHILI KI DALEEL HAI AUR MALIK K HUKM ME KAHILI ABDIYAT K MANAFI {Contradiction} HAI.

NOTE- NAMAZ APNE MUQARRAR WAQT PE FARZ HAI ISLIYE USKO WAQT PE ADA KARNA AFZAL HAI AUR KHUDA KO YE PASAND HAI AUR ASHIQ KA MANSAB HI YAHI HAI K EHKAME MASHOOQ K AAGE SARE TASLEEM KHAM RAHE ISLIYE TALEEM-E-WARSIYAT BHI YAHI HAI.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal Warisul Kareem

يٰٓـاَيُّهَا الَّذِيۡنَ اٰمَنُوۡا كُتِبَ عَلَيۡکُمُ الصِّيَامُ کَمَا كُتِبَ عَلَى الَّذِيۡنَ مِنۡ قَبۡلِکُمۡ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تَتَّقُوۡنَۙ

Aye Iman Walo Tum Par Roze Farz Kiye Gaye Hai Jaise Pehle Logo Par Farz Kiye Gaye The Taki Tum Parhezgar Bano

{Surah Baqarah Ayat 183}

Sarkar Aalampanaah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Farmate Hai~

1. SHOK SE ROZA RAKHNA AASHIQO KI SUNNAT HAI.

2. ROZA RAKHNE SE KHUDA KI MOHABBAT BADHTI HAI.

3. ROZA AISI QADR IBADAT HAI KI ROZEDAAR BANDE KO KHUDA APNE DOSTO MAI SHUMAAR KARTA HAI.

4. ROZA RAKHNE SE NAFS MAGHLOOB {Surrender} HOTA HAI.

5. ROZA GUNAHO KO MITATA HAI.

Roze Ka Asal Maqsad Hi Hasile Parhezgari Hai, Allah Yahi Chahta Hai Ke Uske Bande Parhezgar Ban Jaaye Aur Parhezgari Tabhi Hasil Hoti Hai Jab Banda Gunaho Ka Rasta Chhod Kar Uski Taraf Mutawwajo Hota Hai. Yani Jab Banda Gunaho Se Taiyb Ho Jata Hai Tab Parhezgaro Mai Shumaar Hota Hai {Jo Ke Bhut Mushkil Hai Par Na Mumkin Nahi} Aur Gunaho Ka Rasta Banda Tabhi Chor Sakta Hai Jab Uska Nafs Maghloob Ho Jaaye Yani Nafse Ammara Se Rukhsat Ho Kar Uska Safar Nafse Mutmainna Tak Pahuch Jaaye Aur Ye Saari Nemate Usko Roze Ke Zariye Hasil Hoti Hai.Islye Yahi Taleeme Warsiyat Hai Ke Roza Rakhne Se Nafse Ammara Se Chhutkara Milta Hai Aur Fir Khuda Ka Qurb Hasil Hota Hai.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.

Zakat Bade Nafe Ki Tijarat Hai, Jis Maal Ki Sacche Dil Se Zakat Di Jaaye To Wo Maal Har Shar Se Mehfooz Ho Jata Hai, Usme Barkat Hoti Hai.

وَاَقِيۡمُوا الصَّلٰوةَ وَاٰتُوا الزَّكٰوةَ

Aur Namaz Qayam Karo Aur Zakat Ada Karo.

{Surah Baqarah Ayat 43}

Rasulallah ﷺ Ne Irshad Farmaya~

Jisne Apne Maal Ki Zakat Adaa Kar Di Toh Aisa Karne Se Maal Ka Shar Jata Raha { Yani Uska Maal Mehfooz Ho Gaya}

Sarkar Aalampanaah Huzoor Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu Farmate Hai~

1. JIS MAAL KI SIDQ DIL SE ZAKAT DI JATI HAI KHUDA USKE MAAL KA MUHAFIZ HO JATA HAI.

2. BAD BAKHEEL WO HAI JO ZAKAAT NAHI DETA.

3. ZAKAAT KI FARZIYAT SE INKAR KARNA KUFR HAI.

4. KABHI MUTABASSUM LABO SE IRSHAD FARMAYA~ KI ZAKAAT BADE NAFE KI TIJARAT HAI KE 1 RUPAYE KE EVAZ MAI KHUDA DUS RUPAYE AUR BAAZ MAUQE PAR 70 RUPAYE DETA HAI.

5. MASHRABE ISHQ MAI ZAKAAT KI TAREEF YE HAI KE JO HALQ {Throat} SE FURU {Under} HO JAAYE WO APNI THI AUR JO BAQI RAHE WO SAB ZAKAAT HAI {Yani Mashrabe Ishq Mai Aashiq Ke Jo Basic Needs Hai Khana Peena Kapda etc Iske Ilawa Jo Kuch Bhi Extra Bache Aashiqeen Arifeen Use Rahe Khuda Mai De Deta Hai}. Lekin Shariat Mai Maal Ka Hisab Karke Zakat Dena Farz Hai Aur Wo Maal Jispe Pura Ek Saal Guzar Chuka Ho Uska 40va Hissa Yani 2.5 % Zakaat Farz Hai Aur Sahibe Nisaab Par Uss Maal Ko Apne Gareeb Rishtedaar Fir Miskeen Fir Yateem Mohalle Ilaqe Mai Mustahiq Tak Pahuchaye Yahi Farz Hai. Ye Chanda Khor Tanzeemo Ko Jo Zakaat Le Lalchi Log Hai Unko Dene Se Bache.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIU WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.

Sarkar Waris Paak Aalallahu Maqamuhu irshad Farmate hain~

1. Suna Suna Haj chand imtihanat ka majmua hai Jo is me sabit kadam raha , to us ka shumar khuda k dosto me hua.

2. Suna Suna Jisne khuda k bharose par Haj kiya to us ki imdad Ghaib se hoti hai.

3. Suna Suna Jis ne sidq wa khulus se Haj kiya us ka Imaan kamil hai.

4. Suna Suna Haji WO hai jis par Haqeeqate Haj Munkashaf (khul ) ho jaye.

5. Suna Suna Mezabe Rehmat ka paani gunaho ko dho deta hai , agar barish hoti hai to hujaj us k neeche khade hokr nahate hain.

6. Suna Suna Ashiq ka apne Mashooq se mil jaana hi HAJ-E-MAQBOOL hai.

Kisi ne Sarkar Alampanah Huzur Waris Paak Azamallahu Zikruhu se daryaft kiya k Sarkar aap kyse haaji hain, Abhi tak Ehram zebe tan kiye hue hai ,log to haj krte hain aur ehram utaar dete hai.

Sarkar Alampanah ne irshad farmaya~

"SUNA SUNA LOG HAJ KRNE JAATE HAIN KAABE KO CHHOD KAR AATE HAM KAABE KO LEKAR AAYE HAIN"

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Waqiyat

Namaz

Namaz

×

Hazrat Maulana Sirajul Yaqeen Sahab ke bete Hazrat Maulana Sadiqul Yaqeen sahab jo Hazrat Nijatullah Shah Qadri Rahmatullah Alaih ke Gaddi Nasheen the Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ke baare mein accha Gumaan nahi rakhte the. Saath hi apne Shagirdo ko Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki bargah mein jaane se mana karte the. Maulana Sadiqul Yaqeen Sahab Ilm Hasil karne ke liye Baghdad Sharif tashreef le gaye, wahan kaafi saal rahe. Fir wahan se 7 saal Makkah Mukarrama aur Madina Munavvara mein qayam kiya. Fir jab Hindustan aane ka irada kiya toh pehle Deva Sharif Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki khidmat mein hazir hue aur Sarkar ki Qadambosi ki. Log hairat mein the ke Maulana sahab toh Sarkar ke baare mein accha Gumaan nahi rakhte the, fir achanak se Sarkar se itni Muhabbat kaise ho gayi? Jab logo ne Maulana Sadiqul Yaqeen Sahab se poocha toh unhone bataya ke main Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ke Buland Maqam se bey khabar tha. Maine 7 saal tak Masjid-E-Haram aur Masjid-E-Nabvi mein Namaz padhi aur har Namaz mein maine apni Seedhi taraf Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ko Namaz Padhte Paaya. Yeh meri Aankho Dekhi baat hai. Iske baad maine apne Gumaan se Tauba kar li.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.

Jinn

Jinn

×

Baghdad Sharif mein Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ka ek mureed rehta tha jiski Biwi par har Itwar ek jinn sawar hota tha. Bohot Ilaj, dua karne ke baad bhi use koi rahat na mili. Jab Sarkar Waris-E-Paak Baghdad Sharif tashreef laaye toh woh mureed Aapki bargah mein hazir hua aur apni takleef Sarkar se bayan ki aur makan par tashreef laane ki guzarish ki. Ittefaq se woh itwar ka hi din tha. Jab Sarkar uske Makan par Tashreef le gaye toh jinn uss Aurat par Sawar hi tha. Sarkar ne uss Jinn se poocha ke woh kyu har Itwar iss Aurat par kyu Sawar hota hai, toh usne jawab diya ke main iss Aurat ka Aashiq hu, isi liye isse milne har Itwar ko aata hu. Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ne uss Jinn se irshad farmaya ke agar saccha Aashiq hai toh har Itwar ko hi kyu aata hai, har Roz aaya kar. Sarkar ke yeh Qalme sunn kar Jinn ne kaha ke aisa Ishq mujhe Manzoor nahi, main jaa raha hu aur ab kabhi nahi aaunga. Uske baad se woh Aurat bilkul theek ho gayi aur Sarkar ki Mureed ho gayi. Uss waqt Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki Umr Sharif 14 saal thi.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.

Gustakhi

Gustakhi

×

Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ki Azmat-O-Shaukat dekh kar ek martaba khuch Ulema Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ke paas Imtihan lene ki garz se hazir hue. Sarkar ki bargah mein Hazir ho kar unhone kaha ke Aapka toh chehra hi Kaala hai aur aap Faqeeri ki Badi badi baatein karte hai (Naoozbillah Min Zalik). Sarkar Waris-E-Paak ka yeh Dastoor tha ke Aap Nazrein Jhukaye Baitha karte the. Jaise hi Ulema ke yeh Jumle Sarkar me sune Aapne apna Chehra-E-Aqdas utha ke kaha ke tum Kaale, aur fir se Nazre Jhuka li. Usi waqt unn sabki shaqle Kaale Suwar ki manind ho gayi aur 3 din tak unki Halat aisi hi rahi aur 3 din baad sab mar gaye. Unn sab ki Shaqle itni Bigad gayi thi aur itni badboo daar ho gayi thi ke unke ghar wale bhi dekhna nahi pasand kar rahe the. Rasool Allah ki aulad ki shaan mein jisne bhi Gustakhi ki beshaq uska Khatima bil Khair nahi hua hai.

ALLAHUMMA SALLE ALA MUHAMMADIU-WA'ALA AALE MUHAMMADIN WABARIK WASALLIM.

Huwal-Warisul-Kareem.
